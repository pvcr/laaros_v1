About Laaros

Laaros is the ideal service provider for your Birthday and Holiday parties, Business meetings, memorial gatherings or other significant events.

We're a family-owned company. We plan, organize and decorate for events. From weddings and birthday parties to professional conferences. We handle with intense attention to detail and dedication to your requirement.

In addition to planning your event, we offer event materials such as one-of-a-kind gift bags and accessories. Our goal is to make your event a special day. We take great pride in our unique and personalized service to ensure that your event is amazing. We recognize the importance of this day and will provide everything you need to ensure a memorable experience for you and your family. We love when we hear "This is the best party ever" when people leave the party.
